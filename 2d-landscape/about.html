CR!T

I'm creating an online platform where designers can share their approaches, methods and tools to collectively embrace the complexity of critical design and create a collective map to orient oneself in. As a student at DAE, a school that positions itself within critical design, I often find myself confused about what critical design actually does, who a critical designer is and which tools or methods they use. By making this knowledge shared and more accessible, especially for students and designers, I hope to cultivate a space that strengthens the connections within the discipline.
